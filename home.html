Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) is the largest comprehensive university in Malaysia providing innovative education with state-of-the-art infrastructure and technology within reach at its 34 campuses, 4 colleges of studies, 14 faculties, and 9 academic centres all over the country.

Welcome to 2025!

From 1956 until today, through the transformation from RIDA (Rural & Industrial Development Authority) to ITM (Institut Teknologi MARA) and finally to UiTM (Universiti Teknologi MARA), this institution has demonstrated a continuous commitment to providing quality higher education and playing a significant role in the social and economic development of the country.

On August 26, 1999, the Prime Minister of Malaysia at the time, Dato' Seri Dr. Mahathir Mohamad, announced the name change from ITM to Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM). UiTM's aspiration is to become a world-class higher learning institution and the best university.

On August 26, 2024, it marked the 25th anniversary of the transformation from the name of Institut Teknologi MARA to Universiti Teknologi MARA.
